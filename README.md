# 🍔 Online Food Delivery Business Analytics System

> **End-to-End SQL + Power BI Business Analytics Project**  
> Transforming food delivery data into actionable business insights through analytics, KPI tracking, and interactive dashboards.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

The online food delivery industry is highly competitive and data-driven. Companies need to continuously monitor revenue performance, customer behavior, delivery efficiency, and restaurant trends to make better business decisions.

This project analyzes transactional food delivery data using **PostgreSQL** and visualizes insights using **Power BI** to answer key business questions and provide strategic recommendations.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Food delivery platforms face several operational and business challenges:

- Declining revenue trends
- Delivery delays impacting customer satisfaction
- Difficulty identifying top-performing cities and cuisines
- Lack of visibility into customer payment behavior
- Need for customer retention and loyalty insights

This analytics project solves these challenges through KPI-driven analysis and interactive dashboarding.

---

## 🚀 Project Objectives

✔ Measure total revenue and order performance

✔ Analyse delivery efficiency and late deliveries

## 📈 Key Business Insights

### 🔹 Revenue Analysis
- Mumbai is the **highest revenue-generating city**
- Chennai and Kolkata follow closely
- Revenue shows a **declining trend from March to September**

### 🔹 Customer Insights
- Small group of customers contributes a large share of revenue
- Male customers contribute slightly higher revenue
- Repeat customer opportunity exists

### 🔹 Cuisine Analysis
Top performing cuisines:

- South Indian
- Indian
- Italian
- Fast Food
- Chinese
- Desserts

### 🔹 Payment Behavior
Most used payment methods:

- UPI
- Card
- Cash on Delivery (lower share)

### 🔹 Delivery Performance
- Average delivery time = **40.22 mins**
- Indicates opportunity for logistics optimization

## 💡 Business Recommendations
